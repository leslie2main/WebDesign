Welcome to my Moneyball-themed website! Explore the story of how statistical analysis changed the game of baseball.

Chapter 1: The Opening Game

The movie opens with the Oakland A’s struggling on the field. I enjoy this part because it immediately sets the stakes and shows the challenges Billy Beane is facing as a general manager trying to keep his team competitive.

Chapter 2: Discovering Sabermetrics

Billy meets Peter Brand and learns about using statistics to find undervalued players. This chapter is exciting because it introduces the innovative strategy that changes baseball forever.

Chapter 3: Building the Team

The process of recruiting players who are overlooked by other teams is fascinating. I enjoy seeing how careful analysis and creative thinking combine to form a competitive roster.

Chapter 4: The Winning Streak

The team goes on an incredible winning streak, defying expectations. I love this chapter because it shows that unconventional ideas can succeed, and it’s very satisfying to watch the strategy pay off.
