Welcome to my Moneyball fan site! Explore the story of how statistics changed baseball.

Chapter 1: The Opening Game

The movie opens with the Oakland A’s struggling on the field. It immediately sets the stakes and shows the challenges Billy Beane faces.

Billy meets Peter Brand and learns about using statistics to find undervalued players. This chapter is exciting because it introduces the innovative strategy.

The process of recruiting overlooked players is fascinating. I enjoy seeing how analysis and creative thinking combine to form a competitive roster.

Chapter 4: The Winning Streak

The team goes on an incredible winning streak. This chapter is satisfying because it shows unconventional ideas can succeed.
