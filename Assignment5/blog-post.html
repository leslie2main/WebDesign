Ideas for My Next Softball Website

For my next website, I’m thinking about creating something centered around softball, where I can showcase tips, drills, and maybe highlight some favorite teams or players! I want to mix images and text to give a real feel for the sport while practicing layouts, visuals, and simple interactions.

One tip I learned for Flexbox is to focus on one direction at a time — row or column — and then adjust spacing and alignment inside that direction. For Grid, setting up grid-template-columns first helps create a clear structure, then gap, justify-content, and align-items keep everything clean and predictable.
